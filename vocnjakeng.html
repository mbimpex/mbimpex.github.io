Orchard


Considering the importance of fruit quality for products, MB Impeks raised an orchard in 2004. Most of the fruit used in production is sourced from our orchard. The "Circle of love" orchard is located at the base of the Motajica mountain, in the village Nožičkio near Srbac on an area of ​​45 hectares. Annually we produce around 400 tons of William pear, plum sprouts, quince and old varieties of apples.

Food


We welcome organized groups interested in touring the orchard. By appointment we can prepare meals that are characteristic of our region. Highlights include bread, pie and lamb under the sač (such), lamb on a spit, beans from earth pot and grilled dishes with the inevitable Banja Luka ćevap (chevap).

Team building

If you entrust us with the organization of your business meetings and seminars, we are sure that you will come back to us again. We offer you the opportunity for training and education in a beautiful setting. An inspiring room in a relaxed natural environment is the perfect place for new ideas and plans for a better future.

Accommodation

A maximum accommodation at the orchard is for 19 people. Accommodation is in wooden houses with 5 double and 3 triple rooms. Each room has a bathroom and toilet. The houses are made of natural materials and are furnished in the ancient spirit of local tradition. By agreement there is a possibility to expand the capacity.

Entertainment

We are equipped with high-end music devices and other accompanying equipment, and if you are interested we can organize a live music band for you.

Recreation

For those who like an active vacation, there is a sports field for volleyball, football, basketball, table tennis, billiards, darts, table football and cycling through forest roads that can be used for walking and running. There is also a pool in unreal surroundings where you can enjoy the crystal clear water. A place for rest, relaxation and inspirational cocktails from Baba's cocktail bar. The most beautiful setting for morning coffee.
