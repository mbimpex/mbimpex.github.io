Extraordinary cooperation between Coca - Cola HBC and MB Impeks

Prestige and quality of Zlatna brandy and Zlatni Pelin line was recognized by our business partner Coca-Cola Hellenic Bottling Company (HBC). Since 2017, Coca-Cola HBC Serbia is the owner of our premium brands Zlatna brandy and Zlatni Pelin. A cooperation agreement has been signed, within which MB Impeks produces these brands for Coca-Cola HBC Serbia.

Coca - Cola HBC is the official distributor of a complete portfolio of MB Impeks products in Bosnia and Herzegovina. In Serbia, Croatia, Slovenia, Montenegro and Bulgaria, Coca-Cola is the official distributor of most brands.

ZLATNA BRANDY


FOUR MASTERS ONE NAME - ZLATNA

Premium line of natural fruit brandies from the Zlatna brand includes Zlatna Williams Pear, Zlatna Apricot, Zlatna Quince and Zlatna Plum.

We can proudly say that in terms of the quality, they stand, at the very least, side by side with the world's leading producers of fruit brandies. Only a symbiosis of experience, tradition, wealth of natural resources and modern tehnology can result in top quality products such as Zlatna brandy. As a premium product, Zlatna brandies are packaged in packaging that emphasizes all their properties - natural content, distinctive taste, aroma, and their exclusivity and sophistication.


Zlatna brandies are a regional premium brand. They were created for lovers and connoisseurs of top quality alcoholic beverages. They are made of the highest quality fruit. The fruit is mashed, so that pure fruit puree goes into fermentation. Distillation is performed in the old traditional way. They are currently exported to 11 countries on three continents. Zlatna brandies have won many awards, and one of the most prestigious is the Grand Prix of Moscow fee in 2010.


They should be consumed chilled. The concentration of alcohol in the product is 40% vol.


ZLATNI PELIN

Wormwood has long been known as a "medicinal herbal liqueur". Zlatni Pelin is recognizable by the pronounced bitter taste of the wormwood plant, from which the most valuable properties of this miraculous plant are released after months of aging in alcohol. With an addition of various herbs, which enrich it, it gets a specific aroma and a pleasant refreshing scent. Each bottle contains a sprig of wild wormwood. Wormwood we use in production comes to us from Herzegovina (due to its composition and healing properties, it is one of the best in the world).


Zlatni Pelin should be consumed chilled as an aperitif and digestive after a meal. It can also be consumed in combination with ice and lemon.
The concentration of alcohol in the product is 28% vol.
