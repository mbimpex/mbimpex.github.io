Vidarica


We can proudly say that Vidarica is a special drink that brings decades of experience. Immediately on the first sip of our spirit made of herbs, you can feel the special and full taste of the fifteen herbs that are combined in a wine distillate, in which no single ingredient prevails. The multitude of medicinal ingredients possessed by each of the herbs, which together create harmony and a pleasant taste for the palate, was at the same time the inspiration for us to named it Vidarica. It is ideal to drink as an aperitif or a digest.

Baba Višnja

Surely this brand is well known to you, the first association when it comes to toasting with cherry liqueur. Maraska cherry juice is immersed in alcohol in which it releases its incredible aroma and ruby ​​red color. To these components is added premium red wine and then the gentle and sweet Baba Cherry is produced. In addition to the traditional cherry flavor, there is a sweeter raspberry-flavored Baba Mila and a sweet-sour Baba Riba made from currant. Your favorite Baba is moderately chilled alone or as a cocktail ingredient and goes perfectly with dessert.

Meduška


The original recipe for the preparation of chestnut and acacia honey immersed in alcohol results in a refined and full flavor of Meduška. Simply a honey goddess, a drink that blends past and present. The spirit of the past is embodied in the tradition of making honey liqueurs, which even gave the ancient Slovenes vigor and strength in ancient times. Made with your own recipe, Meduška will delight your senses with its mild honey taste and take you on a time-consuming, unforgettable journey.

Walther


The liqueur made according to the original recipe in a combination of medicinal herbs and fruit has a strong aroma and a pleasant scent that provides an incredible experience. The strong and unique taste of Walther consists of a dose of uncertainty and mysterious happiness. When it will, it will.
